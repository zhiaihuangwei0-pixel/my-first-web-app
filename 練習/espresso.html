Espresso

What is Espresso?

Espresso is a concentrated form of coffee served in small, strong shots and is the base for many coffee drinks.It is made by forcing pressurized hot water through finely-ground coffee beans using an espresso machine.The result is a rich, full-bodied coffee with a layer of crema on top, which is a golden-brown foam that adds to the flavor and texture of the espresso.
