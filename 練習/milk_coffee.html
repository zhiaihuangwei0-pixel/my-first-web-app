Milk Coffee

What is Milk Coffee?

Milk coffee is a coffee beverage that contains milk as a primary ingredient. It is made by combining brewed coffee with steamed or heated milk, often with a layer of foam on top. Common types of milk coffee include cappuccinos, lattes, and macchiatos, each with varying ratios of coffee, milk, and foam.
